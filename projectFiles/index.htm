FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment?

Simply fill out the booking form above with your details, preferred doctor/specialty, and availability. We'll confirm your appointment via email within 24 hours.

Can I cancel or reschedule an appointment?

Yes, you can cancel or reschedule up to 24 hours before your appointment time. Instructions will be included in your confirmation email.

Is telemedicine available?

Many of our healthcare providers offer telemedicine consultations. Please indicate your preference when booking or contact us directly.

How do I pay for my appointment?

Payments are processed securely through our platform after your appointment. We accept all major credit cards and some insurance plans.
